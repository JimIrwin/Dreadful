This page at BIKEXCHANGE has reviews of bike computers. I suspect you want wireless (avoid running a wire up the front forks from the sensor to the computer), which would rule out the cheapest options. But there seem to be good options for $100 or less (with added costs if you want heart-monitor functionality?) For urban use, I bet you want it easily removed. Otherwise, I'm agnostic on key parameters such as: battery or USB-chargeable; small or large display; specific features ...

There are some promising options from Trek,which is the brand I used when I was cycling as cross-training for running.
